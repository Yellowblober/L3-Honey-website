The histroy of Beekeeping

From ancient Egypt to modern farms, beekeeping has shaped human civilization in surprising ways.

  • 10,000 BC

    Early Beekeeping

    Humans began harvesting honey from wild bees.

  • 3,000 BC

    Egyptian Beekeeping

    Ancient Egyptians kept bees in cylindrical hives.

  • 17th Century

    Movable Comb Hives

    Innovations in hive design allowed for easier honey extraction.

  • 19th Century

    Langstroth Hive

    Revolutionary hive design with removable frames.

  • 20th Century

    Modern Beekeeping

    Advancements in protective gear and hive technology.
